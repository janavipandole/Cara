How We Ship

Every Cara order is carefully packed and collected by our courier partners within 1 to 2 working days of your purchase. Once your parcel is on its way, we will send a tracking link by email so you can follow it all the way to your door.

Delivery windows below start from the date your order leaves our warehouse, not from when you place it.

Order Processing

  • Orders placed before 2:00 PM (EST) on a weekday are usually dispatched the same day
  • Orders placed after 2:00 PM or over the weekend are sent the next working day
  • A confirmation email is sent right after your purchase is complete
  • A dispatch email with your tracking number follows once your order leaves our warehouse

International Shipping

We deliver to over 35 countries. All international parcels include tracking. Please note that local customs may hold shipments for inspection — this is outside our control.

  • Import duties and taxes are set by the destination country and are the buyer's responsibility
  • Cara cannot influence customs processing timelines
  • Some items may be restricted in certain regions — check local rules before ordering

Tracking Your Order

  • Your tracking link arrives in the dispatch email and goes live within 24 hours of sending
  • US orders are tracked through FedEx, UPS, or USPS depending on your chosen method
  • If tracking shows no updates for over 5 working days, contact us and we will investigate
