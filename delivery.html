📦 Shipping Policy

We are committed to delivering your orders as quickly and safely as possible.

Shipping Method Estimated Delivery Cost
Standard Shipping (US) 3 – 5 Business Days Free on orders above $50
Express Shipping (US) 1 – 2 Business Days $9.99
International Shipping 7 – 14 Business Days Calculated at checkout
Same-Day Delivery (select cities) Same Day $14.99
ℹ️ Shipping times are estimates and may vary during peak seasons or due to courier delays outside our control.

⚙️ Order Processing

Once your order is placed and payment confirmed, our team begins processing immediately:

  • Orders placed before 2:00 PM (Mon–Sat) are processed the same day.
  • Orders placed after 2:00 PM or on Sundays are processed the next business day.
  • You will receive a confirmation email with tracking number once dispatched.
  • Processing may take 1–2 extra days during sale events or high-traffic periods.

🌍 International Shipping

We ship to over 50 countries worldwide. Please note:

  • International delivery takes 7–14 business days depending on destination.
  • Customs duties, taxes, and import fees are the responsibility of the customer.
  • We are not responsible for delays caused by customs clearance processes.
  • Some items may not be available for international shipping due to restrictions.
ℹ️ Shipping cost for international orders is calculated at checkout based on your location and order weight.

📍 Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you can track it anytime:

  • A tracking number will be emailed to you upon dispatch.
  • Use the tracking number on our Track My Order page.
  • Tracking updates may take up to 24 hours to reflect after dispatch.
  • For any tracking issues, please contact our support team.

↩️ Returns & Exchanges

We want you to love every purchase. If something isn't right:

  • Returns accepted within 30 days of delivery.
  • Items must be unused, unwashed, and in original packaging with tags attached.
  • Sale or discounted items are not eligible for return unless defective.
  • Exchanges are subject to stock availability.
  • Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after we receive the returned item.

For full details, see our Terms and Conditions.
